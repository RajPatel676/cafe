A journey
in every cup.

We believe that a meal is more than just food; it's a passport to new cultures and experiences. 365 The Travel Cafe was born from a passion for exploring the globe and bringing the finest culinary discoveries back home to Surat.

From carefully sourced beans roasted to perfection, to globally inspired dishes prepared with local, fresh ingredients, we put our heart into everything we serve. It's an environment where every sip and bite feels like a mini-vacation.
