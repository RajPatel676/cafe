Our Story
A cafe for dreamers

More Than
Just a Cafe

365 The Travel Cafe is a destination for travelers, dreamers, creators, and coffee lovers. Every corner is designed to inspire conversations that last long after the last sip.

From Himalayan trek photographs on the wall to a world map made of coffee beans — every detail transports you somewhere new.

Our all-vegetarian menu draws from global flavors: Italian pastas, Mexican wraps, Middle Eastern hummus bowls, and exceptional coffee.
